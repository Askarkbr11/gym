==========================================
HOW TO ADD YOUR CLIENT PHOTOS
==========================================

Your client photos need to REPLACE the placeholder images.

LOCATION: public/assets/images/

QUICK STEPS:
1. Convert your photos to WebP format
2. Name them exactly as listed below
3. Copy them to: public/assets/images/
4. Replace the existing files
5. Run: npm run dev

REQUIRED FILES:
- hero-fallback.webp (reception/exterior photo)
- gym-interior.webp (wide gym view)
- personal-training.webp (trainer with client)
- trainer-1.webp (trainer photo 1)
- trainer-2.webp (trainer photo 2)
- trainer-3.webp (trainer photo 3)
- testimonial-1.webp (member photo 1)
- testimonial-2.webp (member photo 2)
- testimonial-3.webp (member photo 3)
- testimonial-4.webp (member photo 4)
- transformation-1-before.webp (before photo 1)
- transformation-1-after.webp (after photo 1)
- transformation-2-before.webp (before photo 2)
- transformation-2-after.webp (after photo 2)
- transformation-3-before.webp (before photo 3)
- transformation-3-after.webp (after photo 3)
- transformation-4-before.webp (before photo 4)
- transformation-4-after.webp (after photo 4)
- transformation-5-before.webp (before photo 5)
- transformation-5-after.webp (after photo 5)
- transformation-6-before.webp (before photo 6)
- transformation-6-after.webp (after photo 6)
- testimonial-bg.webp (background texture)
