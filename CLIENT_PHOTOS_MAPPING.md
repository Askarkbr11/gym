## 🎨 Image Selection Tips

1. **Hero:** Choose the most impressive, branded image
2. **Trainers:** Use clear, professional portraits
3. **Transformations:** Ensure clear before/after contrast
4. **Testimonials:** Use happy, authentic member photos
5. **Interior:** Show the gym's best features and equipment
